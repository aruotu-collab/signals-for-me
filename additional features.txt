That's the key insight.

Google answers "What are you looking for?"

News answers "What happened?"

Bloomberg answers "What does this mean?"

Your app should answer:

"What opportunity does this create?"

Every article, job posting, funding announcement, regulation, patent, social trend, Reddit discussion, earnings report, or product launch becomes a signal.

The app's job is to convert signals into opportunities.

The App's Real Value

The app should not be a search engine.

It should be an Opportunity Discovery Engine.

Google says:

"Here are 10 links."

News says:

"This happened."

Your platform says:

"This happened. Here are the 7 business opportunities, 3 consumer opportunities, 2 risks, estimated market size, startup ideas, and companies most likely to benefit."

That positioning is much closer to a "Bloomberg Terminal for opportunities" than a traditional news aggregation product.
